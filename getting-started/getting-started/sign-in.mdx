## Sign in to DocMetrics

Go to [docmetrics.io/login](https://docmetrics.io/login).

**Option 1 — Email and password**

Enter your email address and password, then click **Sign In**.

**Option 2 — Google**

Click **Continue with Google** to sign in instantly with your Google account.

---

## Forgot your password?

Click **"Want to set a password or forgot yours? Reset here"** on the login page.

You will be taken to [docmetrics.io/reset-password](https://docmetrics.io/reset-password) where you can reset your password by entering your email address. Check your inbox for a reset link.

---

## Invited to a team or data room?

If someone sent you an invitation link to join their team or data room, click that link first before signing in. DocMetrics will automatically redirect you to the correct place after you log in — you do not need to do anything extra.

---

## Having trouble signing in?

- Make sure you are using the same email address you signed up with
- Check that your caps lock is off when entering your password
- If you signed up with Google, use the **Continue with Google** button instead of email and password

Still stuck? Contact us at [support@docmetrics.io](mailto:support@docmetrics.io)
