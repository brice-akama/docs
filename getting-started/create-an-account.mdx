## Create your account

Go to [docmetrics.io/signup](https://docmetrics.io/signup) to create your free account.

You have two ways to sign up:

**Option 1 — Email and password**

Fill in the following fields:
- **First name** — your first name
- **Company name** — the name of your business or organisation
- **Email address** — use a work email for best results
- **Password** — choose a strong password

Click **Get Started** to continue.

**Option 2 — Google**

Click **Sign up with Google** to create your account instantly using your Google account. No password needed.

---

## The 4-step setup

After entering your details, DocMetrics walks you through a short setup to personalise your experience.

**Step 1 — Your details**
Enter your name, company, email and password.

**Step 2 — Your industry**
Select the industry that best describes your business. Options include Legal, Real Estate, Financial Services, Technology, Healthcare and more.

**Step 3 — Your team size**
Select how many people work at your company — from just yourself up to 500+ people.

**Step 4 — How you plan to use DocMetrics**
Select everything that applies to you:
- Board and investor updates
- Raising capital
- Selling a product or service
- Employee onboarding
- Client or partner relationships
- Merger or acquisition
- Raising funds for a company

You can select more than one. Click **Continue** when done — your account is created and you are taken straight to your dashboard.

---

## Free trial

Every new account starts with a **14-day free trial** of the Pro plan. No credit card is required.

- Your trial starts the day you sign up
- You will receive a reminder **3 days before** your trial ends
- You will not be charged unless you choose to upgrade
